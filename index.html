Welcome to the GitHub repo for the LACASIST Website.

The LACASIST (Los Angeles Chapter of the American Society for Information Science and Technology) website runs on Drupal and is hosted on Dreamhost. A regional chapter of an international non-profit organization for information professionals, we strive to make our website collaborative. Of course, being a volunteer-based group, it will take us a while to get there.
